France: Practitioners Exhibit Some of the Torture Methods Used by the Jiang Group to Persecute Falun Gong (Photos)

July 26, 2004 |  

(Clearwisdom.net) On July 17, 2004, French practitioners gathered at one of the biggest Chinese districts, located in the 13th zone of Paris, and used a live theatrical approach with pictures to expose the persecution of Falun Gong conducted by the criminal Jiang group. The Jiang regime attempts to force Falun Gong practitioners to give up their belief in Truthfulness, Compassion and Tolerance by employing various inhuman torture methods. These methods include the "Tiger Bench," being locked in a cramped cage, harassing, force-feeding, injecting nerve-damaging drugs, slave labour, bamboo under fingernails and many more.

The live torture exhibition deeply touched both the Chinese and French population. It was hard for them to believe that in the 21st century, behind the 'culture year' celebration, Jiang coerced the whole country to commit brutal crimes against innocent people.

People sign the petition against the persecution

A gentleman looked at the banner reading "Bring Jiang Zemin to justice." At first he doubted very much whether it was possible to bring the dictator to justice. After practitioners' truth clarification with strong righteous thought, he agreed that Jiang Zemin should be punished just like other dictators have been and wished practitioners all the best.

Calling on people to learn about the persecution

People come to take Falun Dafa materials one after another and sign the petition against the persecution of Falun Gong.

On July 18th, practitioners clarified the truth to visitors from all over the world in the Human Rights Square opposite the Tower.
