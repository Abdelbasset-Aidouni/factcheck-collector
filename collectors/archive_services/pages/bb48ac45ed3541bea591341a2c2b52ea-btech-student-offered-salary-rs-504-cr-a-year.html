B.Tech Student Offered Rs 5.04 Crore Annual Package

Jalandhar: Microsoft offered a huge salary package of 5.04 crore per anum to a girl student of Lovely Professional University (LPU).

According to the reports, Tania Arora a fourth-year B. Tech CSE student of LPU has received a huge salary offer from Microsoft. Microsoft with a package of Rs 42 lakh per month (Rs 5.04 crore per anum). After joining her job, Tanya will be working as a software engineer at Microsoft India R&D Center in Hyderabad.

LPU Chancellor Ashok Mittal said they were very proud of Tanya for her achievement. LPU students have held record jobs for the past three years. LPU students find jobs in companies such as HP, Amazon, Yahoo, Cisco, Apple and Google, which are listed on Fortune 500 companies.
