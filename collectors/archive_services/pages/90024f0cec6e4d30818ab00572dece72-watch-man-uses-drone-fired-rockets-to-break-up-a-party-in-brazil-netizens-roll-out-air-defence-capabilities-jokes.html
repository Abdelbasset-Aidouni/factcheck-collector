WATCH: Man Uses Drone-fired Rockets To Break Up A Party In Brazil, Netizens roll Out 'air Defence Capabilities' Jokes

Written By Koushik Narayanan | Mumbai | Published: July 16, 2019 11:32 IST

Hack:

  • A man in Brazil used a drone loaded with fireworks to break up a party that was causing a lot of noise, the video of which has gone viral
  • In the video grab of the incident, one can see the drone taking off and instantly raining fireworks at different directions as party goers scramble to escape from the attack
  • The video, which was posted on YouTube, saw a huge number of views, extending its popularity all around the world

Tech innovation reached dizzying new heights when a man in Brazil used a drone loaded with fireworks to break up a party that was causing a lot of noise. Visuals of the incident have surfaced on social media, following which the incident has gained enormous popularity. 

In the video grab of the incident, one can see the drone taking off and instantly raining fireworks at different directions as party-goers scramble to escape from the attack. The man who initiated the attack did so as he wanted to put an end to a party happening in the locality as loud music was annoying him. 

Here's the video grab of the Star Wars like incident:
