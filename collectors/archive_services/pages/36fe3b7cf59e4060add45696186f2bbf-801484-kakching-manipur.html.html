Overview

Kakching is a Municipal Council city in district of Thoubal, Manipur. The Kakching city is divided into 12 wards for which elections are held every 5 years. The Kakching Municipal Council has population of 32,138 of which 15,710 are males while 16,428 are females as per report released by Census India 2011.

Population of Children with age of 0-6 is 4181 which is 13.01 % of total population of Kakching (M Cl). In Kakching Municipal Council, Female Sex Ratio is of 1046 against state average of 985. Moreover Child Sex Ratio in Kakching is around 927 compared to Manipur state average of 930. Literacy rate of Kakching city is 83.08 % higher than state average of 76.94 %. In Kakching, Male literacy is around 90.21 % while female literacy rate is 76.40 %.

Kakching Municipal Council has total administration over 7,144 houses to which it supplies basic amenities like water and sewerage. It is also authorize to build roads within Municipal Council limits and impose taxes on properties coming under its jurisdiction.

Kakching Caste Factor

In Kakching (M Cl), most of the (M Cl)rs are from Schedule Caste (SC). Schedule Caste (SC) constitutes 87.57 % while Schedule Tribe (ST) were 1.30 % of total population in Kakching (M Cl).

Kakching Work Profile

Out of total population, 14,867 were engaged in work or business activity. Of this 8,458 were males while 6,409 were females. In census survey, worker is defined as person who does business, job, service, and cultivator and labour activity. Of total 14867 working population, 84.61 % were engaged in Main Work while 15.39 % of total workers were engaged in Marginal Work.
