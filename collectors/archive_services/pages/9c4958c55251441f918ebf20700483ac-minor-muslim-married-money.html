16-Year-Old Muslim Girl Married Off To A 65-Year-Old Arab Man For Money

Updated on 28 August, 2017 at 7:21 pm By Bhagyashree Pancholy

A 16-year-old Muslim girl has been married off to a 65-year-old Arab man for Rs. 5 lakh in Hyderabad.

The elderly man from Oman paid Rs. 5 lakh to get married to a minor girl in Hyderabad. The girl’s parents have now approached the police alleging that the girl was fraudulently married to the sexagenarian Omani national. The teen girls parents have also approached the city’s commissioner to help them get the girl back from Muscat, where she currently resides after her marriage.
