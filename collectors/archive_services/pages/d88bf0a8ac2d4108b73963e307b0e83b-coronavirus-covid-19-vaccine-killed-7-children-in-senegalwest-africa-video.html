    Just In: COVID-19 vaccination in Senegal(West Africa) has claimed the lives of 7 children, which is so pathetic.

    News coming out from the province of Africa states that the COVID-19 vaccine which was meant to prevent, and combat coronavirus in the system has started claiming lives, ezone57.com reported. P8

    COVID-19 vaccine testing was announced to start today, 8th April 2020 in West Africa.

    When this information reached the Africans, some of them rebuked it and called the whites ‘enemies of Africa’, because, they thought it’s the intention of the whites to vandalize Africa with flattering.

    As some of the African leaders refusing the vaccine testing in their countries, Senegal president accepted that the vaccine is tested in his county.

    The mass vaccination was started just this morning and the first 7 children who received it died on the spot.

    This created their awareness.

    The masters in charge of the vaccination process are all arrested by the state police service and are being detained for investigation.
