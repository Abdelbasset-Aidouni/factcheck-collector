Anurag Kashyap's Eye-Bandage Experiment Sends Media Into a Frenzy!

Wednesday, 26 August 2015

The director was up to some mischief...find out what!

Anurag Kashyap

Read more about Anurag Kashyap

Anurag Kashyap is a director known for his eccentricities. His personal life has always been under the media scanner, including his much publicised divorce with actress Kalki Koechlin, which allegedly annoyed him quite a bit.
 
He posted a picture of himself on his Instagram account sporting an eye plaster, with a caption “Now they put a plaster, this is what happens when you get into a brawl with a MMA fighter (sic).”
 
Several news websites reported the story with different headlines; the point being his getting into a fight with someone. Anurag later clarified that the pictures were clicked after a prosthetic test and he posted them to prove his point - that the Indian media is always in a rush to fill pages with random news without getting its facts right.
