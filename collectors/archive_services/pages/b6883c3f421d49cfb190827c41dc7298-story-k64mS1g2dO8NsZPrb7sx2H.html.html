PM Modi takes oath in Delhi, his mother cheers him on TV from Ahmedabad

Photographs circulating on social media showed her overjoyed and clapping when Modi appeared on the TV screen.

india Updated: May 30, 2019 23:23 IST
Press Trust of India
Ahmedabad
Prime Minister Narendra Modi's mother Hiraba watches him on television as he takes oath of office and secrecy for the second term, at her residence in Gandhinagar, Thursday, May 30, 2019. (PTI photo)

Like millions of Indians, Hiraba Modi, nonagenarian mother of Prime Minister Narendra Modi, was glued to the TV screen Thursday evening to watch her son taking the oath of office.

Hiraba, 95, watched the ceremony from her house at Raysan near here.

Photographs circulating on social media showed her overjoyed and clapping when Modi appeared on the TV screen.

She lives with Pankaj Modi, the prime minister’s younger brother.

The prime minister visited her on May 26 to take blessings after the BJP’s resounding victory in the Lok Sabha polls.
