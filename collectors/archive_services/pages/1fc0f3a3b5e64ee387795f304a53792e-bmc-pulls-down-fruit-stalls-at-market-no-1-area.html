BMC pulls down fruit stalls at Market No 1 area

Twin City
By pragativadinewsservice On Jan 25, 2020
106
Share

Bhubaneswar: A squad of Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation (BMC) conducted an eviction drive at the Market No 1 area in Bhubaneswar.

According to sources, 15 fruit stalls and a cabinet were demolished during the drive. The BMC officials pulled down the shops situated between the stretch of Raj Mahal Chhak to AG square using JCBs.
