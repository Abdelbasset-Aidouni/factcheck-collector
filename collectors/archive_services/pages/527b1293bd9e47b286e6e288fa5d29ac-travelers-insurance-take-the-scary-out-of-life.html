TRAVELERS INSURANCE : Take the scary out of life.

1
By Kirbs in : BEST ADS // juin 26 2010

Vivre sans la peur du danger, tel est la devise de cette campagne pour Tavelers Insurance. On se retrouve donc dans la savane africaine entouré d’animaux les uns plus dangereux que les autres.

This commercial featuring baboons, lions, zebras, crocodiles, vultures and all sorts of other animals getting along famously at what would normally be a very dangerous watering hole, is the first spot in the new ‘Take the scary out of life’ campaign from Travelers Insurance.
