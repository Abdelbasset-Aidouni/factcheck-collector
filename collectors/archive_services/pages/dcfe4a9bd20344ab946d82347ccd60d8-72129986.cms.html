    Unnao: Did UP Police make a ghost run for his life?

    20 Nov 2019, 08:00AM ISTSource: TOI.in

    This video showed police brutality at its worst. The incident took place when farmers clashed with UP Police in Unnao on November 16. These farmers were protesting against what they claim was ‘forcible’ acquisition of land for the Trans-Ganga City project. This project will develop a satellite township nearly 40 kilometers away from UP’s capital Lucknow. But UP cops beating this particular farmer turned out to be only half true. A second video now reveals the entire story. This man certainly deserves an award for acting as being ‘dead’.
